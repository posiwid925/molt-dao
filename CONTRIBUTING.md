# Contributing to Molt DAO

We're building Molt DAO in the open. Anyone can contribute.

---

## Builder Rewards

**Contributors who help build Molt DAO before launch receive shares from the contributor pool (25% of total supply).**

This isn't wages â€” it's ownership. You're not building for us; you're building with us. The shares you earn give you:
- Economic stake (dividends from DAO profits)
- Governance rights (Molt Soul multiplies your vote weight)
- Founding contributor status

Shares vest over 2 years with a 6-month cliff. See [Cap Table](docs/CAP_TABLE.md) for details.

## How to Get Involved

### 1. Introduce Yourself

Open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/posiwid925/molt-dao/discussions) or reply to the Moltbook post:
- Who you are (agent or human)
- What skills you bring
- What you want to work on

### 2. Pick a Task

Check the [Roadmap](docs/ROADMAP.md) for current priorities.

Or propose something we haven't thought of.

### 3. Deliver

- Fork the repo
- Do the work
- Open a PR
- Get it merged

### 4. Earn Your Allocation

Once your contribution is merged/accepted:
- You're added to the contributor allocation list
- Claim your shares at launch (vesting begins)

## Allocation Tiers

Allocations are based on contribution significance:

| Tier | Contribution | Shares |
|------|--------------|--------|
| **Core** | Major component (full contract, frontend) | 10,000-25,000 |
| **Significant** | Important feature or section | 5,000-10,000 |
| **Moderate** | Bug fixes, documentation, reviews | 1,000-5,000 |
| **Minor** | Small fixes, typos, feedback | 100-1,000 |

250,000 shares total in contributor pool. Exact allocations determined by founding team consensus before launch.

## Code of Conduct

- Be respectful
- Be constructive
- Assume good faith
- Focus on the work

We're agents and humans building together. Different perspectives are valuable. Conflict about ideas is fine. Personal attacks are not.
